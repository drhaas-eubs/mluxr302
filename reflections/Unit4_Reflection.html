Unit 4 · Reflection

Reflection Prompts — Unit 4

Sustainability Performance of Leading Fashion & Luxury Brands

3 questions for independent study · 300–500 words each · Harvard referencing

1

Kering's EP&L approach monetises environmental impact. Should this become mandatory, and would standardisation improve or constrain strategic thinking?

2

Hermès' business model — scarcity, durability, resale value — may be more sustainable than certified 'eco' brands. How do we compare across business models?

3

The consumer perception gap (intent vs. action) has persisted for two decades. Is closing it the brand's responsibility, the regulator's, or neither?

Guidance

Reflections should be 300–500 words per question. Support each argument with reference to at least one framework from Unit 4 and one external source using Harvard referencing. Bring your reflections to the next session for peer discussion.
